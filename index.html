From Beijing to Shanghai: Great Wall ridge walks, pandas and hotpot, neon river nights, glass bridges, and a lot of chili.

Beijing — Great Wall & Temple of Heaven

Day 1–2

Kick-off in the capital: a day trip to the Great Wall for ridge-line views and watchtowers, then the tranquil Temple of Heaven the next morning—cypress groves, bright imperial halls, and locals practicing tai chi.

Zhangjiajie — Glass Bridge, Avatar Mountains & Yellow Dragon Cave

Day 5–7

Surreal Wulingyuan landscapes: crossed the glass‑bottom Zhangjiajie Grand Canyon Bridge, then hiked among the 'Avatar' sandstone pillars in the national park—mist curling around the spires. Finished at Huanglong (Yellow Dragon) Cave with enormous caverns and stalactites.
